# Cafe Rio Tomatillo Dressing

**Score: 8.2/10**

## Ingredients
- 1 packet of Hidden Valley Ranch mix (Traditional)
- 1 cup mayonaise (Hellmans)
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tomatillos diced
- 1/2 bunch of cilantro
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 lime juiced
- 1 jalapeno

## Cooking
- Blend all of these ingredients together, chill and serve.
